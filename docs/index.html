mitma-sedona

Multi-criteria liveability index over Catalonia. Where could I live well — within bike-reach of a train to Barcelona, climbing & yoga nearby, close to green or sea, away from heavy industry, urban heat, and noise.

Apache Sedona 1.9 Spark 4.1 H3 res 8 Lonboard · pydeck · deck.gl MITMA v2 + OSM + EEA + Landsat

Explore the geo-browser →

A dark, satellite-backed map of the liveability index: 45,220 H3 res-8 hexes over Esri imagery, with a preset selector, an analytic-metric recolour, a 2.5D extrude toggle, and MITMA OD arcs + OSM amenity inputs. Dev-scope prototype — sparse coverage in places; liveability is a relative index, not a guarantee.

Demos

Synthetic preview (no backend) →

Standalone deck.gl page exercising H3HexagonLayer (extruded score), ArcLayer (MITMA OD from BCN), ScatterplotLayer (climbing & yoga POIs), with layer toggles, sliders, and hover tooltips. Runs on synthetic Catalonia data, so it renders without any backend.
